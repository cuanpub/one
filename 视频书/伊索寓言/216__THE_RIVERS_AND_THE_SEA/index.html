The Rivers And The Sea

Once upon a time all the Rivers the action of the Sea in making their waters salt. When we come to you, " said they to the Sea, "we are sweet and : but when once we have you, our waters become as and as your own. The Sea replied shortly, "Keep away from me and you'll remain sweet.
