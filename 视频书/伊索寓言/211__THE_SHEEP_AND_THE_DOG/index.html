The Sheep And The Dog

Once upon a time the Sheep about the difference in his treatment of themselves and his Dog. Your conduct, " said they, "is very strange and, we think, very . We provide you with and and milk and you give us nothing but grass, and even that we have to find : but you get nothing at all from the Dog, and yet you feed him with tit-bits from your own table. Their remarks were by the Dog, who spoke up at once and said, "Yes, and quite right, too: where would you be if it wasn't for me? would steal you! would eat you! Indeed, if I didn't keep constant watch over you, you would be too even ! The Sheep were to acknowledge that he spoke the truth, and never again made a of the regard in which he was held by his master.
