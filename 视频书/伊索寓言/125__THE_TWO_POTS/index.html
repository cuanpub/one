The Two Pots

Two Pots, one of and the other of , were carried away down a river . The Pot urged his to keep close by his side, and he would protect him. The other thanked him, but him not to come near him on any account: "For that, " he said, "is just what I am most afraid of. from you and I should be broken in pieces.

Equals make the best friends.
