The Slave

A Slave, being with his lot, ran away from hismaster. He was soon missed by the latter, wholost no time in mounting his horse and settingout . He came up with him, and the Slave, in the hope ofavoiding capture, andhid himself there. Aha, " said his master, "that's the very place for you, my man!
